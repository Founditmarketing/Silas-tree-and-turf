The Silas Difference.

Silas Tree and Turf is a full-service tree-care company with over 20 years of experience in the arboriculture industry.

Owner Silas Holtzclaw is highly trained and skilled at maintaining the health and beauty of your trees. We understand that removing a tree can be a daunting task, which is why our skilled arborists assess the tree to determine the safest, best method of removal for your property.

The trees and yards on the Eastern Shore cause people to stop and admire the lusciousness of our landscape. It takes highly skilled people to preserve this beauty, and Silas Tree and Turf is proud to be part of that preservation.
